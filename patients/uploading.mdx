# Uploading Documents

MedSpace supports a wide range of medical data formats. You can upload files directly into any patient folder.

## Supported Formats
- **Medical Imaging:** DICOM (`.dcm` files or folders).
- **Documents:** PDF Reports, Word Docs.
- **Media:** Clinical Images (JPEG, PNG), Audio Recordings.

## How to Upload

### Method 1: Context Menu
1. Click the **3-Dots (â‹®)** menu on the target patient folder.
2. Select **Upload Files** (for PDFs/Images) or **Upload DICOM** (for imaging studies).
3. Select the files from your computer.

### Method 2: Drag and Drop
1. Open the patient folder so the **Middle Panel** shows the file list.
2. Drag files from your computer desktop directly into the Middle Panel.

<Note>
  **Smart Processing:** When you upload a DICOM folder, MedSpace automatically detects it as a medical study and prepares it for the Viewer.
</Note>

## Bulk Uploads
To upload multiple studies or a large archive, use the **Upload DICOM** option. This ensures that series and study metadata are preserved correctly.
